About Me

I have spent 5 years with Fujitsu Consulting India Pvt. Ltd. and 2 years with Mitsui O.S.K. Lines (MOL), where I worked closely with Japanese stakeholders and global teams.

I hold a formal academic background in Japanese language studies from Visva-Bharati University, which has provided me with a strong foundation in Japanese language, culture, and business communication. My professional journey has enabled me to develop expertise in Japanese-English communication, documentation, interpretation, coordination, and cross-cultural support.

With a deep understanding of Japanese work culture and business etiquette, I aim to bridge communication gaps and add value to organizations operating in Japan-related or global business environments.
